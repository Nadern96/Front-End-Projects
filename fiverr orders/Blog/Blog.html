Great Career Paths for Data Science

Big data is everywhere. And the need for skilled data scientists to analyze, wrangle, and learn from the data to solve unique problems and discover hidden insights is growing. IBM predicts that by 2020 the number of data science job openings with reach more than 2.7 million.

Data Science and Analytics are no longer just buzzwords- they are essential business tools. Research by Andrew McAfee and Erik Brynjolfsson from MIT states, “companies in the top third of their industry in the use of data-driven decision making were, on average, 5% more productive and 6% more profitable than their competitors.

The demand for the data scientist skillset and the wide availability of big data opens up a myriad of career paths. There are many jobs in fields making a huge contribution to the world. Here are some of my favorites:

  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Biotechnology
  • Energy
